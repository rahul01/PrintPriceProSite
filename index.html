Price Calculation Results

Material Cost: ₹0.00
Material Cost = (Material Weight / 1000) × Material Price per kg
Electricity Cost: ₹0.00
Electricity Cost = (Printer Power in Watts / 1000) × Print Time in hours × Electricity Rate per kWh
Labor Cost: ₹0.00
Labor Cost = (Labor Time in minutes / 60) × Labor Rate per hour
Overhead: ₹0.00
Overhead = (Material Cost + Electricity Cost + Labor Cost) × (Overhead % / 100)
Subtotal: ₹0.00
Subtotal = Material Cost + Electricity Cost + Labor Cost + Overhead
Markup: ₹0.00
Markup = Subtotal × (Markup % / 100)
Final Price: ₹0.00
Final Price = Subtotal + Markup
